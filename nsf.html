Our Purpose
Our Mission

The Ninjas STEM Foundation runs competitive robotics teams, builds STEM learning programs for the community, and provides free intellectual property support to young inventors. Three programs. One mission: turn curiosity into creation — and make sure every student has the tools to take it somewhere real.

01 — Compete
🤖
We Show What Young Engineers Can Do

Our competitive robotics teams — VEX IQ Team 276C and V5RC Team 91276C — don’t just participate. They reach World Championships, set division scoring records, and prove that middle schoolers from Frisco can compete at the highest level. Competition is our classroom without walls.

02 — Teach
🏠
We Bring STEM to the Community

The STEM Academy runs workshops, after-school programs, summer camps, and community events for kids from Kindergarten through 8th grade. Robotics, coding, AI literacy, engineering design — we bring it directly to schools, libraries, and community spaces across Frisco. No barriers. No prerequisites. Just curiosity.

03 — Invent
🔬
We Open Doors Most Kids Never Find

The Innovation Lab supports student researchers, science fair participants, young inventors, and early-stage entrepreneurs — including free patent application filing, patent landscape searches, and early-stage business guidance. An idea worth building is an idea worth protecting and growing. We make sure no young inventor or entrepreneur has to figure that out alone.

04 — Grow
🌍
We Build Something That Outlasts Us

Every team member we train, every workshop we run, every patent we help file is an investment in a STEM community that keeps growing long after any single season ends. We are not building a club. We are building a foundation — one that the next generation of engineers, inventors, and leaders can stand on.

Compete. — Teach. — Invent. — Grow.

STEM Academy
Community • Workshops • After-School • Summer Camp • Grades K–8

From hands-on community workshops to structured after-school learning and summer camps, the STEM Academy brings robotics, coding, AI literacy, and engineering design to kids across Frisco. 🔬
Innovation Lab
Research • Science Fairs • IP Support • Entrepreneurship • Grades 5–12

A mentored program where students identify a real problem, design a solution, and take it as far as their curiosity leads — whether that’s a science fair, a patent application, or a first business idea. We support independent research, maker projects, design challenges, and student-driven inquiry. And for young inventors and entrepreneurs ready to take their ideas seriously, we open doors most students never knew existed.

⚖ Pro Bono IP & Entrepreneurship Support

We offer free intellectual property and entrepreneurship support to young inventors and entrepreneurs — including patent application filing, patent landscape searches, prior art analysis, invention disclosure guidance, and early-stage business development advice. If you have an idea worth building or protecting, we can help you understand your options and take your first real steps.
