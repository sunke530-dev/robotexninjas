Five questions for you and your family to think about before we meet. There are no wrong answers — just be yourself!

Hey! We are so excited you are interested in joining the Robotex Ninjas family. We are a VEX IQ championship robotics team expanding into V5RC and building the Ninjas STEM Foundation — a community dedicated to STEM education, robotics, and inspiring the next generation of engineers. Take some time as a family to reflect on these questions before your interview. Come ready to share your stories!
Question 01  —  Work Ethic
What is something you are really proud of that you worked hard to accomplish?

It doesn’t have to be about robotics or technology — it just has to be something that truly mattered to you. Tell us your story.

Your notes
Question 02  —  Teamwork
Think about a time you worked on something with other kids. What was your role, and how did the team do together?

A school project, a sport, a game — anything counts. We want to know how you show up for others and what kind of teammate you are.

Your notes
Question 03  —  Self-Motivation
Is there something you love doing on your own — creating, building, writing, making videos, organizing — just because you enjoy it?

Our team needs people who are curious and get things done even when nobody tells them to. What do you do when it’s all up to you?

Your notes
Question 04  —  Family Commitment
👪 For the whole family
Our team has practices, competitions, outreach events, and community activities — many on evenings and weekends. As a family, how do you handle busy schedules, and what does your support look like?

We want every family to feel prepared and excited, not surprised by the time involved. Your support makes all the difference.

Your notes
Question 05  —  Vision
If you joined our team, what is one thing you would love to learn or do that you can’t do right now?

We are not just building a robotics team — we are building future engineers, communicators, and leaders. We want to know what excites you about this opportunity.
